Login into your New Relic account as Admin. Click on user account at top right corner. Click Account settings

Copy URL


You have now setup your Webhook as an Alert channel. Below steps on Server policies explain how the Webhook channel can be added to receive alerts from Servers. Click on the Server policies under ALERTS which is on left side panel as shown.


Mouse over on your server policy of your choice. Then, click on the Edit button as shown.


Click on the Alert channels tab which is on the right side of the page. Then, click Select channels button as shown.


In the pop-up window, select your configured webhook from Webhook section for receiving Server policy alerts. Then, click Save now as shown.
